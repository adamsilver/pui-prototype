Subject: Register to use HMCTS professional services

From: HM Courts and Tribunals Registrations <hm.courts.and.tribunals.registrations@notifications.service.gov.uk>
Sent: 18 Oct 2018, 11:31am

You've been invited to register your organisation to use HMCTS professional services. You'll be able to:

  • apply for divorce, financial remedy or probate online
  • check the status of a case - if you applied online
  • invite other people in your organisation to use HMCTS professional services
  • check your organisation's fee account balance and recent transactions

What you need to do now

You need to register your organisation.

You'll only need to do this once for your whole organisation. If you do not have the authority within your organisation to do it, forward this email to someone who has.

At the same time as you register your organisation, you'll give us details to set up your own user account.

After we approve your registration, we'll send you an email asking you to finish creating your account. You'll need to click a link in the email and create a password.

After you've finished registering

Once you have an account, you'll be able to sign in to use HMCTS professional services to:

  • manage cases - including applying online
  • manage your account - including inviting other people
