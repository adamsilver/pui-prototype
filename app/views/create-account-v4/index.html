Subject: Register your organisation for HMCTS professional services

From: HM Courts and Tribunals Registrations <hm.courts.and.tribunals.registrations@notifications.service.gov.uk>
Sent: 18 Oct 2018, 11:31am

You've been invited to register your organisation for HMCTS professional services. You'll be able to:

  • apply for divorce, financial remedy or probate online
  • check the status of a case - if you applied online
  • check your fee account balance and transactions

What you need to do

Register your organisation.

You'll only need to do this once for your whole organisation. If you do not have the authority within your organisation to do it, forward this email to someone who has.

After you've registered

After we approve your registration, we'll send you an email. It will tell you how to finish creating your user account.

Sign in to your account to:

  • manage your cases
  • manage your account - for example, inviting other people to use HMCTS professional services
