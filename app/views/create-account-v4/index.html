Subject: Register to manage civil and family law cases

From: HM Courts & Tribunals Registrations <hm.courts.and.tribunals.registrations@notifications.service.gov.uk>
Sent: 18 Oct 2018, 11:31am

Your organisation has been invited by HM Courts & Tribunals Service (HMCTS) to register to manage civil and family law cases. You'll be able to:

  • create divorce, financial remedy or probate cases online
  • upload documents to progress cases which you created
  • check the status of cases created by other people in your organisation
  • check your organisation's fee account balances and transactions

What you need to do now

You need to register your organisation.

You'll only need to do this once for your whole organisation. If you do not have the authority within your organisation to do it, forward this email to someone who has.

At the same time as you register your organisation, you'll give us details about yourself to start creating your user account.

After you've registered

We'll send you an email after we approve your organisation's registration. This can take up to 3 working days.

The email will include a link so you can finish creating your account. After you've done this, you can sign in to your account to:

  • manage your civil and family law cases
  • manage your account - for example, inviting other people to manage their cases online

When you invite other people, you'll be able to choose what they have permission to do.

Yours

HM Courts & Tribunals Service
