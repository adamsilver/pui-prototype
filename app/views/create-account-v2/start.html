Register your organisation for HMCTS professional services

You've been invited to register your organisation for HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) professional services. You'll be able to:

  • apply for divorce, financial remedy or probate online
  • check the status of an online application
  • invite other people in your organisation to use HMCTS professional services

You'll need to give us information about your organisation and yourself. It will take around 5 minutes.

We'll contact you within 3 working days to confirm that your organisation's registration has been accepted.
