Your organisation has been invited to register for HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS)
online services. After you register, you'll be able to apply online for:

  • divorce
  • financial remedy
  • probate

You'll also be able to invite other people in your organisation to use these online services.

What you need to do

You need to register your organisation. At the same time you'll set up a user account for yourself.

When you need to register by

You need to register by the end of Friday 12 October 2018. After that you'll need to contact
HMCTS to get a new invitation.
