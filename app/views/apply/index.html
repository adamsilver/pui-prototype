Register your organisation for HMCTS online services

After you register for HM Courts and Tribunals (HMCTS) online services, you'll be able to set up users who can apply online for:

  • divorce
  • financial remedy
  • probate
  • civil money claims

Users will also be able to:

  • check the status of applications
  • update applications - for example to provide additional information
  • view a list of their upcoming court appointments
  • manage your organisation's HMCTS payment account - if you give them permission

Before you start

You can only register if your organisation is regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA). You'll need your SRA number.

Registering takes around 5 minutes. We'll contact you within 3 working days, either to confirm your registration or to ask for more information.
