Subject: Register to use HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) professional services

From: HMCTS professional services <professional.services@hmcts.gov.uk>
Sent: 1 Oct 2018, 11:31am

Your organisation has been invited to register for HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) professional services. After you register, you'll be able to apply online for:

  • divorce
  • financial remedy
  • probate

You'll also be able to invite other people in your organisation to use these services.

What you need to do

You need to register your organisation. At the same time you'll set up a user account for yourself, with a password so you can sign in to use HMCTS professional services.

You need to register by the end of Friday 12 October 2018. After that you'll need to contact HMCTS to get a new invitation.
