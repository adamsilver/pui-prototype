Subject: Register to use HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) professional services

From: HMCTS professional services <professional.services@hmcts.gov.uk>
Sent: 1 Oct 2018, 11:31am

You've been invited to register your organisation to use HM Courts and Tribunals Service (HMCTS) professional services. You'll be able to:

  • apply for divorce, financial remedy or probate online
  • check the status of a case - if you applied online
  • invite other people in your organisation to use HMCTS professional services
  • check your organisation's fee account balance and recent transactions

What you need to do

You need to register your organisation. You'll only need to do this once for your whole organisation.

If you are not authorised to do this, you can forward this email to someone else in your organisation.

After we approve your registration, you'll get an email asking you to finish creating your account. You'll need to click the link in the email and create a password.

After you've created your password

Once you've created your password, you'll be able to sign in to:

  • create and manage cases
  • manage your account - including inviting other people in your organisation
