Share a case with a colleague

You can use this service if you've made an online application for:

  • divorce
  • financial remedy
  • probate
  • civil money claims

You'll be able to:

  • see who the case is already shared with
  • share the case with a colleague - they'll be able to view and edit it
  • remove someone's ability to view and edit the case
