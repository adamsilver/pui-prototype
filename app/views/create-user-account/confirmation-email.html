You can use HMCTS professional services straight away to apply for divorce, financial remedy
or probate online. You'll also be able to check the status once you've made an application.

You can also manage your HMCTS account if you want to:

  • change your user profile - for example if you change your name
  • invite other people in your organisation to use these online services
  • change your organisation's details - for example adding an address for a new office
  • see a list of transactions and the balance for your HMCTS fee account
