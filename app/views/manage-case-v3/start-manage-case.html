Manage your civil and family law cases

This service allows people in registered organisations to:

  • create divorce, financial remedy or probate cases online
  • upload documents to progress cases which you created
  • check the status of cases created by other people in your organisation
  • check your organisation's fee account balances and transactions

What you can do depends on the permissions you've been given.

Manage your organisation details

If you have permission to manage your organisation details you may be able to:

  • invite other people to manage their cases online
  • check your organisation's fee account balance and transactions

What you can do depends on the permissions you've been given.

If you do not have an account

Someone in your organisation will be able to invite you, as long as the organisation is registered to manage civil and family law cases.

Otherwise, you'll need to wait until your organisation has been invited to register by HMCTS.
