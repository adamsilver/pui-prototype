Manage your civil and family court cases

If you have an account, you can sign in to:

  • apply for divorce, financial remedy or probate online
  • check the status of a case - if you applied online
  • upload documents to progress the case

Manage your organisation details

If you have permission to manage your organisation details you can:

  • invite other people from your organisation
  • check your organisation's fee account balance and recent transactions

If you do not have an account

Someone in the organisation will be able to invite you, as long as your organisation is registered to manage civil and family court cases.

Otherwise, you'll need to wait until your organisation has been invited to register by HMCTS.
