Manage your account

You can also manage your HMCTS account if you need to:

  • invite other people in your organisation to use HMCTS professional services
  • check your organisation's fee account balance and recent transactions

If you do not have an account

Someone in the organisation will be able to invite you, as long as your organisation has been registered to use HMCTS professional services.

Otherwise, you'll need to wait until your organisation has been invited to register by HMCTS.
